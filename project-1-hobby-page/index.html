TV Shows, Animes & Movies

In this web page I'll be discussing some of my most enjoyed programs I've ever come across in my life - one for each genre of visual media. They will range across a multitude of a platforms and genres, the page will evaluate what the production has done well, what it could have done better in my own opinion and a star rating for each one I'll discuss.

Although Anime is considered an Art Style, it's still considered as one of the forms of Visual Media and therefore will be discussed in this page.
