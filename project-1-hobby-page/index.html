Into the Mind of Light Yagami

Who is Light Yagami? A light Overview

In this web page I'll be analysing the protagonist/anti-hero of the Anime series, Death Note. Light Yagami was a honourary student ranking top of all his classes in Japan and son to a member of the Japanese Task Force. In the year 2006, he found the mysterious "Death Note" which appeared out of no where - a book that claimed to take the life of anyone who's name is written.

Not only is he academically gifted, but also shows himself to be highly perceptive with a set of amazing problem solving skills - he was praised and respected highly amongst his family and peers which laid the foundation for an intense game of Cat and Mouse with the Japanese Law Inforcement.

He will later be reffered to as Kira (Killer) or Kami (God ) - a testament to the difference of perception between the public and the Law Inforcement as well as showing the viewer/reader how Light himself perceived his own character. I will be exploring both of these names to describe his sense of justice and how the consequences of these actions align with how "justice" is portrayed in the Anime Series.
