Into the Mind of Light Yagami

Who is Light Yagami? A light Overview

In this web page I'll be analysing the protagonist/anti-hero of the Anime series, Death Note. Light Yagami was a honourary student ranking top of all his classes in Japan and son to a member of the Japanese Task Force. In the year 2006, he found the mysterious "Death Note" which appeared out of no where - a book that claimed to take the life of anyone who's name is written.

Not only is he academically smart, but also shows himself to be highly perceptive with a set of amazing problem solving skills - he was praised and respected highly amongst his family and peers which laid the foundation for an intense game of Cat and Mouse with Law Inforcement.
